Note not all listed is complete, alot of ideas are still to be implemented and or improved! Some are marked as such.

Notable major differences and design decisions:

- New content:
    - Too many new custom scripted interactions because I am addicted to making custom scripts to challenge myself!
        - using a wand to locate rare ores and structures
            - Some structure based progression alongside it! All of the locatable structures have something used for progression
        - Endermen bartering! (Like piglins)
        - Summoning rituals with inworld effects
        - Flutter farming to replace silk moths
    - Custom multiblocks featuring more mechanics than just a bigger grey box with hatches using Multiblocked2 [One so far to integrate embers better into progression, with many planned!]
    - Entirely remade magic progression [WIP]
    - Reworked/replaced several mastery shards.
        - Along with expanding upon them and their concepts, making sure at least each shard fits 2 mods or a fair amount of items into their categories.
    - Newer Create... and plenty other updated mods like Ars of course!
    - Apotheosis nerfed, reworked, reconceptualized [WIP]
        - New bosses only being based on strong/complex enemies you can't so easily get away with cheesing, like iron's spells mages. [Foundation layed out]
        - Higher boss mage tiers spawn as you progress through the magic side of progression
        - Apotheosis bosses no longer drop tiered items, but rather their respective materials so you can reforge and perfect your own crafted items.
        - Everything about it is being tweaked to not be as comically strong, but still enough for a fun customization system.
    - Gear overhaulled! [WIP]
        - Typical enchantable items requiring more effort and magical touches to craft [WIP]
            - They would also be upgradeable into other pieces, so you don't have to worry about losing all your enchants or cool apothic affixes
        - Tinkers construct is the tech route for armor, using their new armor system
        - Removed the mechanical crafting for armor lol [Done]
    - A much expanded questbook, attempting to keep the mistique of E6E's quests not giving much info and being more lore/flavor focused, while still giving enough to go off of so it's not annoying.
        - Questbook is also re-layed out! Removing the not so accurate main gates chapter, and attempting to make quest dependencies make alot more sense.
    - Introducing a Tips quest tab with purchasable tip quests!
        -  Costing materials to unlock information on how to do something for those who may be stuck with something, or feel like they could be doing it better.
            - (No this will not replace base mod guides!) All without needing to visit the discord and ask around (or maybe we don't have a tip for that and you ask anyway, could make a new tip!)

- Major Mod changes:
    - RS replaced with Ae2 and addons, although Ae2 will progress very differently. [Concept mostly implemented, requires refining and playtesting]
    - Astral sorcery removed, new magic progression points using Embers and Wizards reborn. [WIP]
    - Atum is unavailable and I'm frankly not a fan, it has been replaced with the Aether and some addons.
        - Comes with a new slightly different aspectus shard system, replacing the godshards and being integrated into more recipes. [Done]
    - Away with drawers and iron chests to make room for sophisticated storage! [Done]
        - Storage in general is alot easier, with an early very strong crafting station and cheaper chests [Done]
    - Better end is not present
        - a lack of native betterend and an offputting sinytra leads to its replacements with new custom content
            - E.g. silk moths are replaced with alex mobs flutters which can be sheared or killed for lush silk to make brilliant fibers 
    - Tetra removed
        - I am not the biggest fan of it personally and it's pointless to fit a third gear route IMO with the new concepts
